Internet Pizza for Sale! 2.23.2015

Proffessor Evans revealed today that a market place for purchasing pizza with pointcoin has just launched. Anonymous sources at office hours on Friday revealed that the first Papa John's pizza was delivered to them for an undisclosed amount.

Right now, three different types of pizza are available for purchase from the site. Evan's warned today that given the profusion of pointcoin recently the price for that pizza will be rising. The real operator of the site has yet to reveal himself, but it is suspected that Dave himself is financially backing the operation. More to come...

PointCoin is Dead. Long Live PointCoin! 2.15.2015

After network outages and numerous forks, teaching staff decided to restart the PointCoin block chain with better rules on Wednesday of this week. The new block chain will have a higher difficulty and a longer block time to prevent the sort of outages that occured last week.

A soft launch of the new PointCoin network was completed on Monday and it has been opened up for students to join since then. At the time of this writing Point Safari is reporting the new chain faithfully. It is yet to be determined if this new network is more resilient than the last.

Influx of Miners Coming! 2.9.2015

Professor Evans' issued a dire warning today to those who wait to start collecting coins. Since the difficulty of mining readjusts to the number of miners working on blocks, the longer a miner waits to start the harder it becomes to collect coins.

The TA's echoed this concern, but acknowledge that unforseen, calamatous events on the network will readjust the required coin threshold in the student's favor. More news to follow.

The Network is Live! 2.2.2015

The dashing TA's for UVA's CryptoCurrency Cafe course launched PointCoin, an entirely unique digital cash system, last night. The digital currency is dedicated to creating a fixed peg between points on Project 2 and coins held by account holders enrolled in CS 4501.

Staff had several reasons for establishing their own currency. The main goal was to give students tangible exerpience interacting with these new digital cash schemes at the network level. They also wanted students to mine these coins using their own hardware.

When questioned further the instructor admits that other goods will be redeemable with PointCoin. Exactly what goods are for sale is yet to be revealed.
